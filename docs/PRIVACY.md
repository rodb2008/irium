# Privacy Policy

Irium does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The software operates as a peer-to-peer blockchain node. It connects 
to other nodes on the Irium network to sync blocks and broadcast 
transactions. No user data, analytics, telemetry, or identifying 
information is collected or sent to any central server.

No cookies. No accounts. No tracking. No third-party services.
